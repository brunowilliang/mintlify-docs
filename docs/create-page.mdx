<Steps>
<Step>

#### Create Page

Tap the `Create Page` button to start creating your new fundraising page.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Select Page Type

Choose `Simple Fundraiser`:

**Simple Fundraiser**  
A standalone fundraiser for any purpose.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Choose Causes

**Who are you fundraising for?**

Search and select the causes you want to support. You can select up to 5 different causes.

<Callout>
Donations will be split evenly between all selected causes.
</Callout>

</Step>
<Step>

#### Customise Your Page

Configure your fundraising page with the following options:

- `Banner` - Add a header image for your page
- `Avatar` - Upload a profile picture
- `Title` - Give your fundraiser a compelling name
- `Target` - Set your fundraising goal amount
- `Description` - Explain your cause and why people should donate
- `Page Close Date` - Set when your fundraiser ends
- `Theme` - Choose the visual style for your page

</Step>
<Step>

#### Create & Activate

Tap the `Create` button to finish. You'll receive an activation email to activate your fundraising page.

<Callout>
Check your inbox for the activation email. Your page won't be live until you activate it via the email link.
</Callout>

</Step>
</Steps>
