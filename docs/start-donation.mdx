Accept contactless donations quickly and securely using your iPhone's built-in NFC technology.

<Steps>
<Step>

#### Select Your Fundraising Page

Choose the active fundraising page you want to collect donations for from your list of pages.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Accept Tap Donation

Tap the `Accept Tap Donation` button to begin the donation process.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Enter Donation Amount

Select or enter the donation amount you wish to collect from the donor.

<Callout>
You can use preset amounts for faster processing or enter a custom amount.
</Callout>

</Step>
<Step>

#### Enable Tap to Pay (First Time Only - iOS)

<Callout>
Tap to Pay on iPhone is exclusive to iOS devices with NFC capability (iPhone XS and newer).
</Callout>

If this is your first time using Tap to Pay, you'll see a tutorial on `Enable Tap to Pay on iPhone`. This one-time setup includes:

- Video tutorial showing how Tap to Pay works
- Step-by-step instructions
- NFC positioning guide
- Security and privacy information

Complete the tutorial to enable the feature. Once finished, you won't see this tutorial again.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Initiate Tap to Pay

Tap the `Tap to Pay on iPhone` button to activate the NFC reader and prepare your device to accept payment.

<Callout>
Your iPhone is now ready to read the donor's contactless card or device.
</Callout>

</Step>
<Step>

#### Accept the Payment

The Tap to Pay screen will appear. Instruct the donor to:

- Hold their contactless card or device near the top of your iPhone
- Keep it steady until the payment is processed
- Wait for confirmation

The iPhone will vibrate and display a checkmark when the payment is successfully read.

</Step>
