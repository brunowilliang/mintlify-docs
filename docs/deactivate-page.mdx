<Steps>
<Step>

#### Access Page Edit

Navigate to your `Active Pages` list and tap the edit icon (pencil) next to the page you want to deactivate.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Scroll to Bottom

Scroll down to the bottom of the edit screen where you'll find the deactivation option.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Deactivate Page

Tap the `Deactivate Page` button. A confirmation alert will appear.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Confirm Archive

Tap `Archive` in the confirmation alert to complete the deactivation.

<Callout>
Your page will be moved to `Inactive Pages`. You can view all deactivated pages by switching to the `Inactive Pages` tab.
</Callout>

</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**What Happens After Deactivation?**

- The page is archived, not permanently deleted
- It will no longer appear in your Active Pages list
- You can find it in the `Inactive Pages` tab
- Donors will no longer be able to access the page
</Note>
