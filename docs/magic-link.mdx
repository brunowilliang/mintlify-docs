#### Enter your email

Enter your `Email` address in the field provided.

<Step>

#### Tap Send Magic Link

Tap the `Send Magic Link` button to receive your login link via email.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Check your email

Open the email and tap the magic link to instantly log into iDonate Tap.

</Step>
</Steps>

  No need to remember passwords! Magic links are secure, expire after a short time, and can only be used once.
