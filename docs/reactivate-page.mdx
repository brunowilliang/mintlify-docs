<Steps>
<Step>

#### Access Inactive Pages

Tap the `Inactive Pages` tab to view all your deactivated fundraising pages.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Browse Inactive Pages

Review all your inactive pages and find the one you want to reactivate.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Select Page to Reactivate

Tap on the page you want to reactivate. A confirmation alert will appear.

<Callout>
The system will automatically extend your page for 1 month from today. You can edit the close date later if needed.
</Callout>

</Step>
<Step>

#### Confirm Reactivation

Tap `Reactivate` in the confirmation alert to restore your page.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Page is Live Again

Your page is now active and will appear in the `Active Pages` tab. Donors can access and donate to your page again.

<Callout>
Remember to check and update your page details if needed. You can edit the close date, description, or any other field after reactivation.
</Callout>

</Step>
</Steps>
