<Steps>
<Step>

#### Step 1: Basic Information

Enter your `First Name` and `Last Name`. Your URL will be automatically generated, but you can edit it if needed. Tap `Next Step` to continue.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Step 2: Account Details

Enter your `Email`, create a `Password`, and confirm it in `Confirm Password`. Accept the `Privacy Policy` and `Terms and Conditions` checkbox, then tap `Create`.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Success screen

You'll see a success message: "You're almost ready to fundraise!" 

You can:
- Tap `Open Inbox` to open your email client directly
- Or tap `Return to Login Page` to go back

</Step>
<Step>

#### Verify your email

Check your email inbox and click the verification link to activate your account.

</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Email Verification Required">
  You must verify your email address before you can start using iDonate Tap. Check your spam folder if you don't see the verification email.
</Callout>
