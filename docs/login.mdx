<Steps>
<Step>

#### Enter your credentials

Enter your `Email or Username` and `Password`. Tap the eye icon to show/hide your password.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Tap Sign in

Tap the `Sign in` button to access your account.

</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="After Login">
  You'll be redirected to your active pages screen where you can select which fundraising page to use.
</Callout>

## Alternative login methods

- `Send me a magic login link` - Login via email without password. [Learn more](/docs/getting-started/magic-link)
- `Forgot your password?` - Reset your password. [Learn more](/docs/getting-started/forgot-password)
- `Create your account` - Don't have an account? [Sign up here](/docs/getting-started/sign-up)
