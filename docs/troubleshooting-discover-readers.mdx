## Problem

When you start the app while logged in, a modal appears with the message `Discovering...` and either:
- The modal gets stuck and won't close
- An error message appears
- The app fails to proceed to the main screen

---

## Solution

<Steps>
<Step>

#### Close the app completely

Make sure to close the iDonate Tap app completely. Do not just minimize it - swipe it away from your recent apps list.

<Callout>
On iOS: Swipe up from the bottom, find the app, and swipe it up to close it completely.
</Callout>

</Step>
<Step>

#### Reopen the app

Launch the iDonate Tap app again from your home screen.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Check if the issue is resolved

When the app restarts, it will automatically attempt to discover card readers. The modal should close normally and the card reader should be available to accept tap to pay transactions.

</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**Why does this happen?**

The exact cause of this issue is still being investigated as the card reader integration is currently in beta. A fresh app start usually resolves the connection problem.

**If the problem persists:**

Please contact our support team at helpdesk@idonate.ie with details about when the error occurred.
</Note>
