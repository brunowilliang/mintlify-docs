After successfully processing a donation, you can instantly send a receipt to the donor's email address.

<Steps>
<Step>

#### Complete the Donation

Follow the donation process until you see the `Donation Success` screen.

</Step>
<Step>

#### Request Receipt

On the success screen, you'll see the prompt: `Would you like a receipt?` Tap this button to open the receipt sending form.

<Callout>
Sending a receipt is optional. You can skip this step if the donor doesn't require one.
</Callout>

</Step>
<Step>

#### Enter Email Address

A text field will appear asking for the donor's email address. Enter the email address where the receipt should be sent.

<Callout>
Make sure to enter a valid email address. The receipt will be sent immediately and cannot be resent from this screen.
</Callout>

</Step>
<Step>

#### Send Receipt

Tap the `Send` button to deliver the receipt to the provided email address.

The donor will receive a detailed receipt including:

- Donation amount
- Date and time
- Fundraising page details
- Transaction reference

</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**Best Practices**

Pro Tip: Always confirm the email address with the donor before sending to avoid delivery issues.

- Double-check the email address before sending
- Ask donors to add your email domain to their contacts
- Inform donors that the receipt will arrive within a few minutes
- Keep transaction references for your records
</Note>
